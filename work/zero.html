ZERO

Graphic Identity (Rebranding).

Communication Materials & Merchandising (Academic Project).

2022

The development of this project aimed to create the rebrand of the cultural association Zero, in this way an interdisciplinary collaboration was necessary, which brought together several areas of expertise. It took our involvement as designers with the community, and the ability to define, investigate and ultimately come up witha solution that fits the association's needs.

This Project had the collaboration of the designers Margarida Monteiro, Maria Chimeno and Carolina Martins.
