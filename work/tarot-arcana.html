TAROT — ARCANA

Editorial design. (Academic Project).

2022

Creation of a Manifesto on the Major Arcana of the Tarot. This book is divided into several chapters, which covers the roots of the Tarot and the various explanations of the 22 cards that make up the Major Arcana. The book comes in a box with the remaining communication materials, which are, a flag and a Tarot deck composed of 22 cards created and designed from my knowledge and inspiration on the subject.
