ATOM THOUGHT

Merchandising of my identity.

2021 - 2022

Personal Graphic Experiences.

The creation of these graphic pieces all refer to a dream like and fantastic scenario, emocians and feelings, where there is a constant contrast between blacks and vibrant colors. In this way, they have the intention of transmitting the escape from reality to utopian scenarios as a hope for us to bear life with more lightness.
