Erasmus Intership at Lézard Graphique

2024

In 2024 I was lucky enough to work for a few months with the fantastic team of artisans and friends @Lézard Graphique atelier in Strasbourg FR, where I learnt the various techniques of screen printing. I learnt what materials are used, what the whole elaborate printing process is like and what is needed to be sent. I helped realise prints for numerous artists and events and at the end of the internship I realised and printed my own piece.

Here is my visual report/diary of my erasmus in France. Hope you like it!
