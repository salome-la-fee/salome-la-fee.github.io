DNAD — SHARING IS EVERYTHING

Communication Materials (Academic Project)

2023

Sharing is Everything is a project that aims to respond to the penguin statement proposed by DNAD. This kit/ portable library, seeks to celebrate and normalise queerness, openly and proudly whilst creating an opportunity to learn new stories, interact and connect with one another and to build our own identities! This Project had the collaboration of the designers Maria Chimeno.
