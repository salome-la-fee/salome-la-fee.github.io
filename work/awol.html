AWOL - Absent Without Official Leave

Visual Identity.

Communication Materials & Editorial Design (Academic Project).

2023

Absent Without Official Leave it’s a travel agency to the future cities in the year 2121. It aims to reflect on technological developments and the state the state of a utopian ecosystem, which has been tampered with by the emergence of the current climate crises and varied current ecological forecasts and their collateral damage.

Exhibited on "How I Learned to Stop Worrying and Love the Bomb" in Galerias Municipais Pavilhão Branco, June 2023.
