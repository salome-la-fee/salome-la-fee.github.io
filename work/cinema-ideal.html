CINEMA IDEAL — WAYS OF SEEING

Graphic Identity.

Communication Materials (Academic Project).

2020

This project consists of 7 different posters for the (fictional) replacement of the documentary series “Ways of Seeing” conceived by John Berger and produced by Mike Dibb. Each of the posters has an experimental and procedural approach capable of abording different languages and visual references, each one based on a different hierarchy.
