ADEGA BAR 1987

Identity Rebrand Project (Academic Project)

2022 - 2023

The development of this project aimed to recreate the identity of the restaurant/bar Adega, which is a small and very well known space located in Ericeira, by the sea and half an hour from Lisbon. Adega is a tapas restaurant with almost 40 years, it is a place with a very special energy.
