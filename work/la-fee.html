LA FÉE UNIVERSE - HAND DRAWINGS

Painting | Illustration |Risography | Serigraphy

2017 - Present

All the pieces created are mainly handmade from various projects. They are all made using a variety of techniques including watercolours, acrylics, charcoal and even risographs and silkscreens in a variety of sizes. All of them are reminiscent of Salomé's unique and fantastical universe. On the other hand, we can also see some more realistic charcoal portraits, paintings on walls and furniture and even on surfboards.
