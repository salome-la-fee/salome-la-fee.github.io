Graphic and Communication Designer based in Lisbon, Portugal. Rooted in a family where the spirit and cultural interests are very present and have always been used as a basis for everything, but focusing mainly on music. Approaches design and illustration as the answer to his own artistic creation, in a multidisciplinary, fantastical and idyllic way. She is also a multifaceted visual artist, concentrating on editorial work, creating visual identities and illustration within the socio-cultural and artistic sector. Her interest lies in the creative process and in bringing to life everything that can grow outside of the limits of the screen.
