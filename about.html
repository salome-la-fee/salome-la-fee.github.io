Graphic and Communication Designer, based in Lisbon, Portugal. Grew up in a family where the creative spirit, culture in general, and especially the visual arts and music are a daily presence. In a multidisciplinary and whimsical way, she uses design and illustration as a vehicle for her own artistic creation. She is also a visual artist, focusing mainly on editorial work, visual identities and illustration. Her creative process and the discipline to work independently are just as important as teamwork in a project environment where she can learn and contribute. She is also interested in putting into practice anything that can grow outside the limits of the screen.
